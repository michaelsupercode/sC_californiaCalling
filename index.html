Stuck in the middle.

Surrounded by smoke and inspired by the resilience of communities, our design team reimagined the Californian Grizzly and used it to create custom tees, pins and posters.

Our T-shirts (kids and adult sizes), pins and posters will be on sale at our exclusive pop-up shop during the SF Design Week Studio Crawl on Friday 21 June. You can choose what amount to donate. All proceeds will go to the American Red Cross to help the communities that are still displaced.

The Golden Bear

This design is all about the resilience of California to overcome difficult situations and come out stronger than before. It’s a flip of the UNC Tarheel mascot with added elements like the California poppy.

— Kevin Lam, Artist

The Poppy Bear

This design is inspired by the quote ‘grow through what you go through’ and represents the strength of Californians and how we will continue to thrive, regardless of what knocks us down.
